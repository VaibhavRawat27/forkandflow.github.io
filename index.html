Who We Are

Fork & Flow is a student-led open source community designed to help beginners dive into collaborative coding. We empower learners to build meaningful projects, contribute to real-world software, and grow together as developers.

Mission

To make open source accessible and enjoyable for students by providing guidance, opportunities, and a welcoming space for innovation.

Vision

To build a self-sustaining community where students globally collaborate, learn, and lead in open source development and innovation.

Why Join Us?

Get access to beginner-friendly projects, mentorship, workshops, events, and a network of passionate developers like you!
